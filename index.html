01 / About

From execution to strategy. 从执行者到策略人。

I started in the trenches of social media operations, writing, editing, and optimizing content daily. Over five years across media (广告门), brand consulting (念相), and PR (鹰飞公关), I learned the difference between content that performs and content that builds brands.

Today I work as a strategist who bridges the gap between consumer insight and brand expression. My approach starts with emotional truth, moves through rigorous category analysis, and lands on brand roles and content systems that earn their place in people's lives.

I don't sell frameworks. I sell clarity.

我从社交媒体运营的一线做起，每天写、编、优化内容。在广告门(媒体)、念相(品牌咨询)和鹰飞(PR)的五年里，我学会了区分"能跑量的内容"和"能建品牌的内容"。

今天我作为策略师，在消费者洞察和品牌表达之间搭建桥梁。我的方法从情绪真相出发，经过严格的品类分析，最终落在品牌角色和内容体系上——让品牌有资格在人们的生活中存在。

我不卖方法论框架。我卖的是"想清楚了"。

Working Philosophy

工作哲学

"The best brand role is the one that reduces cognitive load, not adds to it." "最好的品牌角色，是减少消费者的心理负担，而不是增加。"
  • 01 Consumer truth first. Everything else follows.消费者的真实感受优先，其他都是推论。
  • 02 Good strategy = consumer-true x brand-ownable x culturally-resonant.好策略 = 消费者真实 x 品牌可拥有 x 文化共鸣。
  • 03 The problem is rarely the one the client came in with.客户的表面需求从来不是真正的问题。
  • 04 In noisy categories, be a companion, not another expert.在高噪音品类中，做陪伴者，而不是另一个专家。

03 / Method

How I arrive at clarity. 我怎么抵达清晰。

Thinking tools built from real client work, codified into reusable frameworks.
从真实项目中提炼的思考工具。
01

The Prism三棱镜推理法

Consumer x Category x Brand. Strategy lives at the intersection.
消费者 x 品类 x 品牌。策略在交汇处。
Most strategies only look at one or two lenses. The Prism forces a three-way convergence, ensuring consumer-true, category-relevant, and brand-ownable answers.
大多数策略只看1-2个维度。三棱镜强迫三方交汇，确保答案在消费者、品类和品牌三个层面都站得住。
02

The Buddy Role品牌搭子角色

In noisy categories, be a companion, not another expert.
在高噪音品类中，做陪伴者，不做另一个专家。
Rejects the brand-as-authority model. Positions the brand as a companion who reduces cognitive load. Best for mass-market brands with high consumer fatigue.
拒绝品牌=权威的竞争惯性，定位为降低认知负担的陪伴者。适合大众消费品类、消费者疲劳度高的品牌。
03

Sit in the Problem First先让客户坐进问题里

Don't pitch the solution. Make them feel the discomfort first.
不要先讲方案。先让他们感受到'不对劲'。
A narrative arc for presentations: present everyday experience, amplify unspoken discomfort, build resonance, then reveal the brand role as relief.
提案叙事弧线：呈现日常体验，放大未被言说的不适感，建立共鸣，再揭示品牌角色作为解决方案。
04

Emotional Truth to Brand Role情绪真相到品牌角色

7-step narrative from consumer emotion to brand permission.
从情绪真相到品牌许可，七步叙事框架。
Industry Mistake, Consumer Fatigue, Evidence, Category Complicity, Brand Permission, Brand Role, Content System, Execution. A complete narrative arc for strategic presentations.
行业误区，消费者疲劳，证据，品类共谋，品牌许可，品牌角色，内容体系，执行。完整的叙事闭环。
