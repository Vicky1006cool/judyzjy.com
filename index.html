About
关于

Strategy before creativity. 先想清楚，再说漂亮。

I'm a brand strategist who believes the best creative work comes from a sharply defined problem — not a blank sheet of paper. Before we make something beautiful, we need to know who we're talking to, what they actually feel, and what role the brand can play in their lives without trying too hard.

With brands like NIVEA, Peninsula Hotel, Aesop, NORDISK, Wrangler, Guming, and Atour, I've built strategies across beauty, hospitality, outdoor, apparel, and F&B — always starting from consumer emotional truth.

I don't sell frameworks. I sell clarity. The frameworks just help me get there faster.

我是品牌策略师。我相信最好的创意来自一个被说清楚的问题，而不是一张白纸。在做出好看的东西之前，我们需要知道：我们在跟谁说话、他们真正感觉到了什么、品牌可以在他们的生活中扮演什么角色——不费力、不端着。

服务过 NIVEA、半岛酒店、Aesop、NORDISK、Wrangler、古茗、亚朵等品牌，在美妆、酒店、户外、服饰、茶饮等多个品类中积累策略经验。每一次，我都从消费者的情绪真相出发。

我不卖方法论框架。我卖的是"想清楚了"。框架只是帮我更快抵达的工具。

Methodology
方法论

How I arrive at the answer. 我怎么抵达答案。

These are the thinking tools I use to move from mess to clarity. Each one was born from real client work, then codified into a reusable framework.
这些是我从"混乱"到"清晰"的思考工具。每一个都来自真实的客户项目，然后提炼为可复用的框架。
01

The Prism三棱镜推理法

Look at the problem through Consumer, Category, and Brand simultaneously. The strategy lives where all three converge.
同时从消费者、品类和品牌三个角度审视问题。策略藏在三者的交汇处。
Most strategies only look at one or two lenses. The Prism forces a three-way convergence — ensuring the answer is consumer-true, category-relevant, and brand-ownable. Used for brand positioning, new product launches, and competitive differentiation.
大多数策略只看了1-2个维度。三棱镜强迫三方交汇——确保答案在消费者层面真实、在品类层面相关、在品牌层面可拥有。适用于品牌定位、新品上市、品类竞争分析。
Brand PositioningStrategy 品牌定位策略框架
02

The Buddy Role品牌搭子角色

In noisy categories, consumers don't need more authority. They need a low-pressure companion.
在高噪音品类中，消费者不需要更多权威，他们需要一个低压力的陪伴者。
The "Buddy" (搭子) role rejects the brand-as-authority or brand-as-celebrity model. Instead, it positions the brand as a companion who reduces cognitive load. Best for mass-market brands in categories with high expert noise and consumer fatigue. Case example: NIVEA's "Your Reliable Buddy" (妮的靠谱搭子).
"搭子"角色拒绝品牌=权威或品牌=明星的竞争惯性。它把品牌定位为一个降低认知负担的陪伴者。最适合大众消费品类、专家噪音高、消费者疲劳程度强的品牌。案例：NIVEA "妮的靠谱搭子"。
Brand RoleConsumer Culture 品牌角色消费者文化
03

Sit in the Problem First先让客户坐进问题里

Don't pitch your solution first. First, make the client re-live the consumer's discomfort.
不要先讲方案。先让客户重新体验消费者感受到的"不对劲"。
A narrative arc for strategic presentations: Present everyday experience → Amplify unspoken discomfort → Build resonance → Then reveal the brand's role as relief. Clients have a natural defense against solutions, but they have no defense against a truth they can feel. Best for complex proposals where the category is noisy or the client is fatigued by sameness.
策略提案的叙事弧线：呈现日常体验 → 放大未被言说的不适感 → 建立共鸣 → 再揭示品牌角色作为解决方案。客户对方案有天然防御机制，但对能感受到的真实"不对劲"没有防御。适用于品类噪音大、客户被同质化提案疲劳的复杂提案。
Deck NarrativePitching 提案叙事Presentation
04

Emotional Truth → Brand Role情绪真相到品牌角色

A 7-step narrative structure: from consumer emotional truth to a brand role that earns the right to exist in their lives.
七步叙事结构：从消费者的情绪真相出发，推导出品牌在消费者生活中"值得存在"的角色。
The sequence: Industry Mistake → Consumer Fatigue → Evidence → Category Complicity → Brand Permission → Brand Role → Content System → Execution. Ensures every strategy deck has a logical, emotionally grounded narrative arc rather than jumping straight to the brand solution.
顺序：行业误区 → 消费者疲劳 → 证据 → 品类的共谋 → 品牌许可 → 品牌角色 → 内容体系 → 执行。确保每个策略提案都有一个逻辑清晰、有情感根基的叙事弧线，而不是直接跳到品牌方案。

Brand Strategy × Content System
NIVEA 妮维雅
"Your Reliable Buddy" — from brand role to annual social content system

→

Brief

Brief

NIVEA needed a 2026 Social Account Operation strategy for the Chinese market. The initial ask was tactical: content calendar, platform operations, KPI targets.

NIVEA需要2026年中国市场Social账号运营策略。初始需求偏向执行层：内容日历、平台运营、KPI指标。

Reframe

问题重构

This wasn't about operating accounts. It was about rebuilding NIVEA's brand role in a market where skincare had become a stressful KPI — and everyone was promising better ingredients.

这不是"运营账号"的问题，而是在护肤KPI化的焦虑市场中，为NIVEA重建一个让人放松的品牌角色。

Insight

洞察

Skincare had become a performance metric — consumers felt they had to learn more, chase faster, choose better, do more complete routines. The result was emotional fatigue: "after you apply it, that's it" — mind-body separation in daily care.

护肤变成了一项KPI——消费者觉得必须学更多、追更快、选更好、做更完整的流程。结果是情绪疲劳："涂完就是涂完了"，身心分离。

Strategy

策略

Positioned NIVEA as "Your Reliable Buddy" — a low-pressure companion who doesn't add to the anxiety but brings care back to simplicity. Built using the Prism method (consumer fatigue × category noise × brand heritage of trust) and operationalized through a full content system, tone guide, and topic architecture.

将NIVEA定位为"妮的靠谱搭子"——一个不增加焦虑、把护理带回简单的低压力陪伴者。使用三棱镜推理法（消费者疲劳 × 品类噪音 × 品牌信任资产）推导，并落地为完整的内容体系、语调和话题架构。

Deliverables

产出物

Strategy deck, annual content system, expression asset library, tone-of-voice guide, competitor social analysis.

策略提案Deck、全年内容体系、表达资产库、语调指南、竞品社交分析。

Result

结果

Proposal approved. This framework continues to guide NIVEA's social content direction.

提案通过。该框架持续指导NIVEA的Social内容方向。

Content Strategy × Social Media
NORDISK
Building a Xiaohongshu presence around "an escape from the everyday"
围绕"逃离日常"建立小红书品牌存在感
→

Brief

Brief

NORDISK, a Danish outdoor lifestyle brand, needed a social media content strategy for the Chinese market, starting with Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book).

NORDISK，丹麦户外生活方式品牌，需要中国市场的社交内容策略，从小红书开始。

Reframe

问题重构

Not "post product content." The real need was: how does a niche outdoor brand create a sense of belonging in a market where outdoor is either luxury gear or extreme sports?

不是"发产品内容"。真正的需求是：一个户外品牌如何在一个把户外等同于"高端装备"或"极限运动"的市场中，创造归属感？

Insight

洞察

Young consumers in the outdoor category are not looking for gear guides. They're looking for an "exit" from the daily grind. The brand that feels like a companion to that escape wins.

年轻消费者在户外品类中寻找的不是"装备攻略"，而是"逃离日常的入口"。能成为这种逃离的陪伴者的品牌，才能赢得他们的心。

Strategy

策略

Buddy Brand Role applied to platform content: content matrix built around "your outdoor buddy" persona, with content pillars across inspiration, education, and community. KOL collaboration strategy and content calendar.

品牌搭子角色在小红书的落地：围绕"你的户外搭子"人设建立内容矩阵，覆盖灵感、教程、社区三个支柱。同时包含达人合作策略和内容日历。

Deliverables

产出物

Social media proposal deck, KOL planning, content calendar, brand introduction deck.

社交媒体提案Deck、达人规划、内容日历、品牌介绍文件。
